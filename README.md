# ReportsTo

> The right manager can change your career. The wrong one can derail it. ReportsTo is how you tell them apart before you start.

---

## The problem

You can research a role, a company, the compensation, the team. And still you walk into a new job knowing almost nothing about the specific person whose decisions will determine whether you thrive or spend two years wondering what happened.

The information exists. It lives in the heads of everyone who has ever worked with that person. There has never been a structured, safe, credible place for it to land.

ReportsTo changes that.

---

## What it is

A verified review platform for individual managers and executives — not companies, not cultures. The specific person you'd be reporting to.

Reviewers verify employment. Anonymity is architectural, not promised. Reviews are structured across observable behavioral dimensions, not free-text. Two reviewer types — direct reports and cross-functional partners — produce complementary, non-substitutable signal displayed separately on every profile.

The core question the product answers: **who is this person, and how do they lead?**

## What we are not building

A complaint platform. An employer review site. A place where leaders can manage, suppress, or purchase visibility over their record.

Trust is earned before it is monetized. Any feature that compromises the integrity of the data destroys the product.
